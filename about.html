ABOUT US

JymJam is a private gym facility based in I-10, Islamabad, Pakistan. After spending 6 years as a personal trainer, Joseph founded the gym so that he could continue to help people transform their lives and achieve their health and fitness goals. Since opening in September 2017, JymJam studio has helped hundreds of people change their lives.


The studio welcomes a range of clients - professional office based workers, teachers, business owners, stay at home mums and more. Whether the goal is to lose a bit of post-baby weight, get in shape for your big wedding day, or improve strength and regain energy, the studio provides it all.


By offering a variety of group and personal training services to tackle personal goals, the unique JymJam experience works for everyone.
